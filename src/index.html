On-chain transactions
Tips are sent through the blockchain directly. This way, the blockchain is used as intended: a backend for electronic cash.
Tiny fees
Bitcoin Cash network fees are close to 0. Deposits, withdrawals and tips below $1 are free. Tips over $1 have a 1% fee sent to the developer.
Instant transactions
All transactions are received as soon as the Bitcoin Cash network processes them, which is almost instant.
Built for Telegram
Currently, this bot works with Telegram. There is a working port for WeChat, and a Discord port is being worked on.
